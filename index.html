Your wrist, Your story

We’re a newly established wristwatch and accessories brand, built on a simple goal to deliver clean, quality, and affordable style for everyone. At Teekay luxe, we believe your wrist should say something, even before you speak.
Our collection includes a wide range of wristwatches from simple, everyday designs to bold, standout pieces that complete any outfit. Whether you’re into classic looks or modern styles, we’ve got something that fits just right.
We also offer bracelets for both guys and ladies cool, stylish, and easy to wear. The kind of accessories that make you feel put together without doing too much.
This is just the beginning for us. We’re growing, we’re building, and we’re excited to bring something different to your wrist game.

Each component is meticulously hand-finished and assembled by our artisans, who dedicate hundreds of hours to perfecting every detail. From the intricate movement to the hand-stitched leather straps, every element reflects our commitment to uncompromising quality.
